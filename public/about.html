THE ARTIST

Hi! I’m Andeng, a passionate creative with a love for art, music, and fashion. My journey has been incredibly inspiring, thanks to the amazing connections I’ve made with local artists, influencers, and brands. These relationships have given me a fantastic kickstart in pursuing my dreams.

I thrive in the local artistic scene of the Philippines, attending local events and collaborating with talented friends who inspire me every day. Their creativity pushes me to carve out my own path and make a name for myself.

In addition to my passion for art, I also love singing and exploring the world of fashion. I believe that creativity comes in many forms, and I’ve always looked forward to express myself through every medium I encounter.

ALL ABOUT TRG

TRG was born from a dream to make a statement in the local creative scene in the Philippines. It all started in April 2023 with the vision of a trendsetting brand that could elevate spaces and connect people to their creativity. After months of planning and preparation, the first tufted rug was officially sold on August 16, 2023.

From sourcing materials to managing finances and delicately tufting each piece, every aspect of the business is crafted with dedication and attention to detail. This hands-on approach ensures the creation of customizable rugs that truly reflect the individuality of each customer.

The mission of TRG is simple: to help people feel more connected to their creativity and uniqueness through custom rugs. Each piece is designed not just for home use, but also to elevate events, photoshoots, and various spaces, transforming any setting into a canvas of art and expression.
